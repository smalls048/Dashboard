Personal Dashboard Pro combines your daily tasks, a Pomodoro timer, ambient focus modes, and live weather into one beautifully modular, distraction-free interface. Because it has no external database, **your data never leaves your computer.**

## ✨ Key Features

* **🌅 Dynamic Time-Based Wallpapers:** Automatically cycles through high-resolution, curated photography based on the time of day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).
* **🧘 Zen Focus Mode:** Clears the screen of all clutter, presenting only your current task alongside a psychological 4-7-8 glowing breathing animation to help you focus.
* **✅ Advanced Task Management:** Support for custom categories, priority levels, recurring tasks, and nested subtasks (checklists).
* **⏱️ Native OS Notifications:** Built-in Pomodoro timer and task due-date alerts that hook directly into your Mac or Windows native notification center.
* **🔀 Modular Workspace:** Swap the layout from left to right, adjust column widths, or hide the scratchpad entirely to suit your workflow.
* **🔒 100% Private:** No cloud sync, no tracking, no accounts. Everything is saved locally to your machine.
